GENERATION MASTERY
White noise provides equal energy across all frequencies - perfect for masking
Pink noise follows natural 1/f distribution - ideal for ambient textures
Brown noise emphasizes low frequencies - great for rumbling foundations
Blue noise increases with frequency - excellent for dithering applications
Use seeds for reproducible results in iterative sound design workflows
Higher sample rates provide better high-frequency detail but increase file size
Stereo width of 0.5-0.7 creates natural soundscape imaging
Amplitude above 1.0 introduces controlled digital saturation effects
Layer multiple noise types at different amplitudes for complex textures
Use violet noise for high-frequency emphasis without harsh aliasing
Red noise provides enhanced low-frequency rumble for drone compositions
Grey noise appears perceptually flat - useful for psychoacoustic testing
