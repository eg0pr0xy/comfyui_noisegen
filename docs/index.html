NOISEGEN

VERSION 1.0.0

"Noise is the unconsciousness of music." - Masami Akita (Merzbow)

NoiseGen is a professional-grade noise synthesis and audio processing suite for ComfyUI, designed for experimental musicians, sound artists, and noise enthusiasts. It combines scientific precision with creative chaos to deliver a comprehensive toolkit for noise music production and sound design.

Scientific Noise Generation

Seven precisely calibrated noise types with spectral accuracy, from classic white noise to organic Perlin textures.

Advanced Processing

Professional-grade effects including granular synthesis, spectral manipulation, and chaos-based modulation systems.

Modular Architecture

18 interconnected nodes for unlimited sonic possibilities, from subtle textures to extreme harsh noise walls.

Real-time Analysis

Comprehensive audio analysis tools with spectrum visualization, waveform monitoring, and statistical metrics.
